Industry Insight

The data reveals that Drama remains the cornerstone of high-rated cinema, representing nearly 29% of the top 1000 list. While the 1990s are often cited as a creative peak, the 2010s saw the highest volume of films entering the top tier, driven largely by the rise of high-budget Action and Animation.
